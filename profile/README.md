## 🌀 About Hacknovate 7.0

**Hacknovate 7.0** is a **30-hour International Hybrid Hackathon** — the flagship tech event of **ABES Institute of Technology (ABESIT)**, Ghaziabad. Inspired by the universe of *Stranger Things*, this edition invites developers, designers, and innovators to step into the **Upside Down of Innovation**, decode the unknown, and build solutions that defy dimensions.

> *Developers and innovators unite to solve real-world problems and turn imagination into reality — driven by creativity, logic, and the spirit of **"Coders Don't Lie."***

---

## 🏫 About ABESIT

**ABES Institute of Technology (ABESIT)**, affiliated with **Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow**, is a distinguished center of technical education in Uttar Pradesh — **Est. 2007**.

Through national-level hackathons, research initiatives, and modern infrastructure, students are empowered to translate ideas into impactful technological solutions.
